⚠ Authorized Use Only
CRITICAL: Every technique in this roadmap must only be practiced on systems you own or have explicit written authorization to test. Unauthorized access is a criminal offense under the CFAA (USA), Computer Misuse Act (UK), and equivalent laws worldwide — regardless of skill level or intent.

Always obtain a signed Rules of Engagement (RoE) document before any engagement. Scope boundaries are legally binding. Practice exclusively in dedicated lab environments (VMs, HTB, THM, home labs). Report discovered vulnerabilities through responsible disclosure channels. Use the MITRE ATT&CK framework to document TTPs during authorized engagements only.
